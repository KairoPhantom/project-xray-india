# Product wedge and 100x thesis

## Category
**Infrastructure Evidence Compiler**, not corruption detector.

## Initial user
A small investigative or civic research team preparing one evidence-heavy infrastructure dossier under deadline.

## One job to dominate
Given a project name and a controlled set of records, produce a review-ready evidence package containing:
- immutable originals and hashes;
- structured project/contract milestones;
- atomic claims with exact page/passage anchors;
- contradictions and official explanations;
- required records not located within a declared search scope;
- reviewer decisions and versioned corrections;
- an export that another person can independently replay and inspect.

## 100x features
1. **Evidence envelope:** every extracted datum travels with source, hash, retrieval event, parser version, page coordinates and confidence.
2. **Contradiction graph:** show conflicting values without silently selecting a winner.
3. **Search-scope ledger:** make “not located” reproducible—where, when and how was searched.
4. **Review diff:** reviewers approve atomic changes, not opaque generated prose.
5. **Claim compiler:** a public sentence cannot render unless its evidence-policy checks pass.
6. **Dossier capsule:** export originals manifest, claims, provenance, review history and checksums as a portable bundle.
7. **Connector observatory:** track portal breakage, field coverage, retrieval success and legal constraints.
8. **Authority response channel:** publish source-backed responses beside disputed claims, never as an afterthought.
9. **Correction transparency:** preserve prior public versions and explain material changes.
10. **Method cards:** every dossier states what the evidence establishes, does not establish and where uncertainty remains.

## Deliberate non-goals
- guilt scores, corruption probabilities or automated accusations;
- autonomous publication;
- anonymous leaking or source protection workflows in v1;
- nationwide crawling before connector governance exists;
- payment movement, escrow or blockchain marketing;
- generic chat over documents as the primary experience;
- replacing legal, engineering or editorial judgment.

## North-star metric
**Median verified claim-hours saved**, where a verified claim includes an inspectable primary source, exact anchor and independent review. Do not optimize raw document count or AI output volume.
