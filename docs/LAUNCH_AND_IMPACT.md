# Launch, traction and impact

## Launch asset
Publish two deeply reviewed dossiers, not hundreds of shallow records. Lead with “what records establish and do not establish,” never sensational guilt claims.

## Initial audiences
- Journalists: faster source-linked research.
- Engineers: technically responsible public explanation.
- Civic/RTI groups: precise document-gap requests.
- Auditors/funders: reusable evidence package.
- Authorities/contractors: source-backed response channel.

## First-week qualified targets
- 5 journalists/researchers complete a workflow.
- 2 engineers review methodology.
- 1 civic organization starts a monitored case.
- 1 authority/contractor response.
- 50 relevant repository stars are more valuable than 10k unqualified visits.

## Impact ladder
`dossier used -> document requested -> response received -> record corrected/disclosed -> formal review -> repair/recovery/policy change`

Track outcomes, not vanity traffic. Publish a monthly resolution ledger.

## Sustainable model
Keep schemas, rules and public core open. Charge for managed hosting, integrations, data conversion, training, assurance workflows and support. Do not hide integrity logic behind a proprietary score.
